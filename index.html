Music Production

This website describes the basics of the process behind the creation of the music we listen to everyday.


Basic Steps of the Music Production

  • Creation
  • Mixing
  • Mastering
Step Involves
Creation Writing, Composing, Recording, Arranging
Mixing Adjusting sounds to fit together within frequency spectrum
Mastering Maximizing and optimizing the perceived volume for said medium of delivery

Note: These steps do not have to be followed as a hard set order and often overlap.


This is a well structured website that breaks the process down into even more steps and goes more in depth into the topic.
